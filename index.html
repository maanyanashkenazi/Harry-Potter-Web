Harry Potter Movies Series

Harry Potter is a series of fantasy novels written by J.K. Rowling. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The movie series contains the plot for 8 movies. In the first movie, "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone", Harry first discovers that he is a wizard, and starts learning at Hogwarts.

Main part of the plot in all the movies is the rivalry between the 4 houses: Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, Hufflepuff, and Slytherin. Each House has their color, their symbolic animal, and unique values.
